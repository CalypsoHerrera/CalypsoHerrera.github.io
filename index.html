Calypso Herrera

I am currently a Postdoctoral researcher in the Stochastic Finance Group at the Department of Mathematics at ETH Zurich. I completed in September 2021 my PhD, Machine Learning in Finance: Applications of Continuous Depth and Randomized Neural Networks, under the supervision of Prof. Josef Teichmann. Previously, I was a quantitative researcher at Finastra in Paris.
