Calypso Herrera

I completed my PhD, Machine Learning in Finance: Applications of Continuous Depth and Randomized Neural Networks, under the supervision of Prof. Josef Teichmann in the Stochastic Finance Group at the Department of Mathematics at ETH Zurich. Previously, I was a quantitative researcher at Finastra in Paris. I obtained my Master in probability and finance at University Paris VI (Pierre et Marie Curie - Sorbonne University) and Ecole polytechnique (Paris) and my Bachelor in mathematics at University Paris XI (Orsay - Paris Saclay University).
